My Experience



I'm a 4th Year Computer Science and Mathematics major at UCSD, with an expected graduation date of April 2021.
During my time here, I've taken advantage of several student leadership opportunities, and also spent some time
honing my technical skills outside of class. Below is a full rundown of my experience at UCSD.



Web Developer Intern: October 2020 - December 2020
Construction Management Association of America

I revamped the organization’s home website with the guidance of a senior web developer using Drupal.




Freelance Web Developer: October 2018 - March 2019
Multicultural Greek Council

I developed websites, using WordPress and Wix, for the events organized by UCSD campus Greek life.
These events mainly consisted of fundraisers, and I also developed stylish homepages for many of the orgs.




Residential Advisor and Assistant: March 2019 - April 2020
John Muir College Residential Life

I Managed Tioga residential hall, which housed about ~1,000 undergraduate students, mostly first years.
Working with a team of 10 RAs, I mediated and resolved all conflicts and disagreements between students on a daily basis,
and organized and hosted multiple weekly events and team-building activities for the residents specifically on my floor, including
trips to the beach, hikes, movie showings, educational presentations and more.



Campus Tour Guide and Ambassador: January 2019 - August 2020
UCSD Admissions Office

After a 10-week intensive training period during which I memorized a 40-page script on details and trivia about the University,
I presented hundreds of walking tours to thousands of tourists and prospective students from all over the world.
When we weren't giving tours, we were involved with student outreach, calling hundreds of current and prospective students
to resolve any questions or issues they had regarding the University. During the pandemic, we handled crisis management
with all of the recently admitted students through these calling campaigns.



Orientation Leader: October 2018 - September 2018
John Muir College

I assisted the staff in welcoming new students and their families, and introduced them to life at the college.
I hosted Q&A sessions answering a variety of parents’ questions and was a positive role model for the freshmen,
teaching them useful skills both in and out of the classroom.
